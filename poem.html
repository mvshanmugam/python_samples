My Summer 2020
A day has gone by, but nobody notices ever...
these are much hard times!
The 'crown' disease has taken over our summer...
I do not know why, at times!

I hope and I hope... that this will soon be over,
But, I'm losing hope!
When this has gone by,
What will happen I wonder... after Covid-19?

Will happier times come again?!
Or will we all still be stuck inside?
As the virus stops spreading,
I look past... at the year 2020.

Be kind! Not cruel... to others and to nature!
Or else, things of such nature
may come haunt us as second nature!!
